## About (2000 chars max)
Orcanaut builds AI agents that actually get work done.

Unlike workflow builders that follow rigid if-this-then-that rules, Orcanaut agents reason through problems, call the right tools in the right order, and adapt when things change. They connect to your existing stack via MCP — Jira, Slack, CRMs, databases — and handle multi-step processes end-to-end.

Define workflows with built-in approval gates. Agents pause for human sign-off on sensitive actions — payments over a threshold, contract changes, access grants — then continue autonomously after approval. You stay in control without micromanaging every step.

Agents collaborate too. A coordinator agent can delegate research to a data agent and reporting to an analytics agent, all orchestrated automatically. Each agent has its own tools, memory, and specialization.

Every run makes them smarter. Agents store learnings in memory, so the task that took 5 steps on day one takes 2 by week three. That's not automation — that's a team member that never forgets and keeps improving.

What Orcanaut does:
- Connects to any tool via MCP (Model Context Protocol)
- Runs multi-step workflows with human approval gates
- Enables multi-agent collaboration — agents delegate to specialized agents
- Learns from every run and gets better over time
- Provides full observability — see every step, every approval, every decision, in real time

We're building for teams that are tired of maintaining brittle automation and want agents that think, not just execute.
